Welcome to MoodFlix!

This is a brief tutorial on how to use the website:

Follow these steps to get the best experience:

  • Select Your Mood: Use the mood wheel to pick how you're feeling today. Whether you're happy, sad, adventurous, or anything else, we've got movie recommendations tailored just for you.
  • Set Your Preferences: Click on the preferences section to customize your experience. You can choose your preferred language and age group. This ensures that the movie recommendations fit your needs.
  • Get Movie Recommendations: After selecting a mood, you'll see a list of movies that match your choice. Click on any movie to get more details, including a brief description and where you can stream it.
  • Shuffle Movies: Not satisfied with the initial recommendations? Use the shuffle button to get a fresh set of movies based on your selected mood.
  • click on streaming link: Click on the streaming link button below the movie card to get redirected to the movie platform.
